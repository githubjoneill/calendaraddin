Active Background Protection

Outlook Agenda Enforcer

This add-in is running in the background to help standardize corporate communications across our organization.

How it works:

  • Every time you send a calendar invite, the add-in checks for a valid agenda in the invitation body.
  • If no agenda is found, sending is temporarily paused, and a template is automatically prepended to the body so you can quickly fill it out.
  • This ensures meetings are productive, well-planned, and highly efficient.

No configuration is needed. You may close this taskpane.
